Gale Canyon Hike Information

  • Hike Rating: Difficult
  • Distance: 7.62mile
  • Elevation Gain/Loss: 1905ft/-269ft
  • Average Slope: 5.9%/-3.1%
  • Max Slope: 14.2%/-9.7%
  • Amenities: Small waystation

Gale Canyon Hike

Start at the Cooperstown Trailhead. Then go through Dingo Gap and the Kimberly. Make your way through the transitions and Mesas. Here you get a good view of the dark dunes. Stop at waystation 11. Waystation 11 is at a gateway to rock gardens and formations. Plan a couple of days of rappelling and rock climbing among the formations.
