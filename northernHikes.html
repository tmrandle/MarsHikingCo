Wavy Formations Hike

Come hike in the northern region of Mars. While there, you can get lost in the geology. These formations will have you going through a maze of Martian sandstone deposits.

Image Credit: NASA/JPL-Caltech/MSSS/JHU-APL/Brown Univ.

Unusual Mound Hike

Come take an easy hike up this unusual mound of dirt and experience the geology of the northern hemisphere. This hike has a small waystation and some good views of the surrounding terrain. This would be a good hike to take to get acclimated to the Martian atmosphere.
