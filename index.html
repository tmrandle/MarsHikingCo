Rover Hikes

Take a hike in areas rich in Martian history. Walk in the rovers path and see the areas where we had some of our first glimpses of what Mars is like. This area is scattered with waystations and settlements.

Image Credit: NASA/JPL-Caltech/Univ. of Arizona

Northern Hikes

Hike the northern region to discover some great Martian geology. There are many areas with layered deposits that invite further investigation.

Image Credit: NASA/JPL-Caltech/MSSS/JHU-APL/Brown Univ.

Southern Hikes

The Southern region is for those who love heights. There are many canyons, craters, and ravines. Rock scrambling, climbing, and ziplines are some of the activities awaiting you.
